Rates of pay will be adjusted within one hundred and eighty (180) days of signature of the Program and Administrative Services (PA) collective agreement. Changes to rates of pay with an effective date prior to the salary adjustment date will be paid according to Appendix “F” of the PA collective agreement, as a lump sum payment. In particular:

  1. Year 1 increases (i.e., “A”): paid as a retroactive lump sum payment equal to a 2.8% economic increase of June 21, 2017, rates.
  2. Year 2 increases (i.e., “B”): paid as a retroactive lump sum payment equal to the year 1 increase plus a 2.2% economic increase, for a compounded total increase of 5.062% of June 21, 2017, rates.
  3. Year 3 increases (i.e., “C”): paid as a retroactive lump sum payment equal to the year 1 and year 2 increases plus a 1.35% economic increase, for a compounded total increase of 6.480% of June 21, 2017, rates.

Pay Notes

  1. An employee being paid at Level AS-7 or AS-8 shall have his/her rate of pay increased
    1. On June 21, 2018, to a rate of pay within the “A” performance range which is two point eight percent (2.8%) higher than his/her former rate of pay, rounded to the nearest multiple of one hundred dollars ($100).
    2. On June 21, 2019, to a rate of pay within the “B” performance range which is two point two percent (2.2%) higher than his/her former rate of pay, rounded to the nearest multiple of one hundred dollars ($100).
    3. On June 21, 2020, to a rate of pay within the “C” performance range which is one point three five percent (1.35%) higher than his/her former rate of pay, rounded to the nearest multiple of one hundred dollars ($100).

Approval date: October 8, 2020.
